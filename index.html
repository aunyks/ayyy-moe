Ayyy Moe!

What's DMV slang really mean?

Translations

Moe

Typically directed toward a friend or aquaintance, "moe" typically means "bro" or "friend" and can be used in place of both words.

Slim

Pretty sure it's the same as moe.

Glizzy

A gun or hot dog. Some say it can also be a penis but idk.

Jhi like

A very esoteric term, "jhi like" can be used to describe assurance, like "you already know", or to emphasize something, like "very".

Jhi

Comes from jhi like. You can use it in place of "very".

On Muvas

Used to express assurance.
